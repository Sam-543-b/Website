Projects

Convective PCR

Designed and developed a centrifugal microfluidic “lab-on-a-disc” (LoaD) platform to automate analysis, breakdown, and testing of biological and chemical samples. Utilizes centrifugal force to eliminate external pumps, enabling low-cost, modular point-of-care diagnostics.

Gel-Filled Additively-Manufactured Lattice Structures

This project focused on enhancing helmet liner performance by developing gel-filled prismatic lattice structures aimed at improving energy dissipation during impact. Traditional EPS foams reduce linear forces but struggle with rotational acceleration—one of the leading causes of traumatic brain injuries. By 3D printing lattice structures from various polymers and filling them with agar-based gel, the study demonstrated that gel-filled lattices significantly outperformed unfilled counterparts in quasi-static compression tests, showing strong potential for next-generation impact protection solutions.
